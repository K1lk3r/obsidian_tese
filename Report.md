#### Synthetic Aperture Radar
sar is a type of Radar technology used for two dimensional high resolution image creation or three dimensional recreation of landscapes. Regardless of weather condition or time of the day. Since it follows RADAR principles, emitting and recording echoes of an eletromagnetic wave weather conditions or time of the day do not have an impact in generating images.




Since it uses eletromagnetic waves both the weather and time of day will not affect the outcome of the image. Since it does not rely on visible light, normally sunlight, it can measure the reflected signals, created before, to build images.
